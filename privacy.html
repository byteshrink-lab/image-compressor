Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how ImageShrink ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our website at image-shrink-theta.vercel.app.

1. No Image Data is Collected

All image compression on this site happens entirely within your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server, never stored, and never transmitted to us or any third party. We have zero access to your files.

2. Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the site (e.g. page views, country, device type). This data is anonymous and aggregated. No personally identifiable information is collected. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

3. Advertising

  • Media.net Privacy Policy

4. Cookies

We do not set any first-party cookies. Third-party services (Google Analytics, ad networks) may set their own cookies as described in their respective privacy policies.
