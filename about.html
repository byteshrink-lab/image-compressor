About ImageShrink

ImageShrink is a free, browser-based image compression tool built for anyone who needs to reduce image file sizes quickly — without installing software or uploading files to the cloud.

We built this tool because most image compressors either require account sign-ups, have file size limits, or send your photos to remote servers. ImageShrink does everything locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

It supports JPEG, PNG, WebP, and HEIF/HEIC formats, includes bulk compression, and lets you download all results as a ZIP in one click.
